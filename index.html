About

Background

I'm a WordPress developer, technical operator, and SEO consultant with 10+ years of hands-on WordPress experience and 8+ years in web design. I build practical, business-ready websites with clean front-end design, custom PHP, reliable plugins, strong SEO structure, and safer WordPress operations.

I founded and scaled RootMyGalaxy.net into a high-traffic Android technology website, then sold it for around $100,000. That experience shaped how I think about content systems: speed, uptime, publishing workflows, search growth, and operational discipline all have to work together.

Today I manage RMG Media Group and oversee the operations department of TheDroidGuru.com, combining WordPress development, editorial systems, SEO planning, performance work, and site reliability.

I also work with clients on custom themes, plugins, landing pages, technical SEO audits, bug fixing, Cloudflare setup, Core Web Vitals, security hardening, and WordPress maintenance.
